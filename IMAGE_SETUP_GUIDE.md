## How to Add Your Profile Image

### Step 1: Save Your Photo
1. Take the photo you uploaded (the outdoor bench photo)
2. Save it as `profile.jpeg` in the `portfolio/images/` folder
3. The image will automatically be cropped to a circle

### Step 2: Optional - Crop for Best Results
For the best look, crop your photo to:
- **Aspect Ratio**: 1:1 (square)
- **Recommended Size**: 800x800px
- **Focus**: Center your face in the frame
- **Format**: JPEG or PNG

### Quick Crop Options:

**Option A: Using Windows Photos App**
1. Right-click your photo → Open with → Photos
2. Click "Edit & Create" → "Edit"
3. Click "Crop & rotate"
4. Select "Aspect ratio" → "Square"
5. Adjust the frame to center your face
6. Click "Save a copy"
7. Rename to `profile.jpeg`

**Option B: Online Tool**
1. Go to: https://www.iloveimg.com/crop-image
2. Upload your photo
3. Select "Square" aspect ratio
4. Crop to center your face
5. Download and rename to `profile.jpeg`

**Option C: Use As-Is**
- Just rename your photo to `profile.jpeg` and place it in `images/` folder
- The CSS will automatically make it circular and apply effects
